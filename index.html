AKAN NAMES

The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born and the order in which they were born. Many of the leaders of enslaved people's rebellions had "day names" including kwasi, kwabena, Kofi, yaw, Kwaku, Abenaa, Yaa, Afua. This names are given according to the day ,month or an year they are born.
